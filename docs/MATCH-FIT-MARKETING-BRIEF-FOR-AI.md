## 1. Executive summary

**Match Fit** is a two-sided fitness matchmaking marketplace that connects **clients** seeking individualized coaching with **independent trainers** who want qualified intent—not cold DMs or static directory listings.

**Tagline (homepage):** “The Perfect Match for Your Fitness Journey.”

**One-liner:** The fitness matchmaking platform that connects clients with the right coaches—and gives trainers tools to grow their brands and client base without the usual friction.

**Category framing:** “The Tinder of the personal training industry” — swipe-based discovery applied to coach–client fit, plus booking, payments, content (FitHub), and AI-assisted matching in one ecosystem.

**Stage:** Public beta (homepage badge). Launch cohort programs limit early slots for clients and trainers with promotional trial/pricing benefits.

---

## 2. Problem & market opportunity

### Client pain points (from product messaging)

- Expensive gym memberships that don’t deliver personalized coaching
- Referrals that fizzle out
- Digging through social DMs hoping someone replies
- Paralysis from static directories: filter once, read walls of text, hope chemistry lands
- Difficulty finding coaches who match goals, personality, communication style, and logistics (distance, virtual vs in-person)

### Trainer pain points

- Discovery logistics burn time and energy
- Unclear intent from prospects (browsing vs serious)
- Outreach limits on other platforms
- Need to build brand between sessions without living on generic social networks
- Fragmented tools for booking, payments, virtual sessions, and content

### Match Fit’s thesis

Combine **swipe-first discovery**, **transparent economics**, **AI-assisted matching**, and **premium coach tools** so clients get momentum instead of paralysis and trainers get qualified intent instead of cold outreach.

---

## 3. Target audiences

### Primary: Clients (B2C)

**Who:** Adults pursuing individualized fitness—rebuilding after injury, performance goals, putting themselves first between work/family, or escaping cookie-cutter gym programs.

**Jobs to be done:**
- Find a coach who “feels like me” before committing money
- Browse full trainer directory and profiles
- Swipe right on coaches they want; swipe left on poor fits
- Book mobile/in-person, virtual, DIY programs, or nutrition support
- Stay engaged via FitHub content and notifications for new local coaches

**Geography:** US-focused (ZIP-based discovery, featured trainers by region, state fields in profiles).

### Primary: Trainers (supply side / B2B on platform)

**Who:** Independent personal trainers, specialists (CSCS, CES, group fitness), and certified nutrition professionals building their own businesses.

**Jobs to be done:**
- Get discovered by serious clients (swipe right = green light)
- Manage interest/inquiries, chat, bookings, and payments on-platform
- Publish services with self-set pricing
- Grow brand via FitHub and optional Premium visibility
- Complete compliance onboarding (background check, certifications, W-9)

**Credential tracks:** CPT personal training, specialist paths, nutrition professional path (required for nutrition offerings).

### Secondary

- **Platform administrators** (internal staff approval workflows—not a marketing audience)
- **Launch cohort early adopters** (limited slots, special trial terms)

## 5. Product features (marketing-relevant)

### Discovery & matching

- Swipe right / left on trainers (Tinder-style deck)
- Scrollable list after filters
- Filters: distance, specialties, algorithmic influence, interests, demographics, etc.
- New trainers added to stack; optional notifications when new coaches available
- AI + algorithms: questionnaire, app usage, chat patterns; optional device signals (disclosed, opt-in)
- Daily client questionnaire (3 questions) feeding matching context
- Trainer “discover clients” batches (premium vs standard tiers differ in volume)

### Client dashboard capabilities

- Find trainers / saved trainers / browse passes
- FitHub feed (like, comment, repost, share trainer posts)
- Messaging with trainers (official chat after match/inquiry acceptance)
- Book sessions (in-person, virtual with integrated meeting links)
- Service management, billing, subscription
- Daily questionnaire, notifications, profile & privacy settings
- Reviews & testimonials for trainers worked with

### Trainer dashboard capabilities

- Onboarding: TOS, W-9, background check, certification uploads (CPT, nutrition, specialist)
- Match / onboarding questionnaire (AI profile text for search)
- Service offerings & pricing (catalog templates: 1:1 PT, small group, nutrition, online program, sport-specific, mobility, HIIT, yoga/pilates-style, etc.)
- Interest clients, client management, coaching notes & goals
- Booking availability, virtual meeting settings (Google/Zoom/Microsoft OAuth)
- Compliance center, billing, Premium Hub / FitHub studio
- Business mileage & expense tracking (tax-prep helpers)
- Promo tokens, featured placement bidding, weekly token grants (premium)

### FitHub (content / social)

- Trainer posts: text, image, video, carousel; hashtags; public or private visibility
- Client engagement drives discovery and trust before booking
- Trainer promotions via tokens for regional feed boost

### Trust, safety & marketplace rules

- User blocks, safety reports, suspensions
- Chat leakage detection (off-platform payment/PII warnings)
- Session punch-in (trainer geolocation at session start)
- Dual-gate payout model (client confirm + trainer complete) with dispute window
- DIY deliverable SLAs and client attestations
- 20% administrative fee on coach service purchases (not on monthly platform subscription)
- Policies keep virtual sessions on approved links in chat (no FaceTime / personal contact off-platform)

### Featured trainers (homepage)

- Regional ZIP-prefix pools
- Paid bid slots + weighted raffle for premium trainers
- Daily allocation displayed on homepage carousel

## 6. Pricing & unit economics (public product constants)

Use these figures in marketing copy only when accurate and approved for the campaign date.

### Client pricing

| Item | Amount | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Platform subscription** | **$10.00/month** | Access to browse full trainer directory and explore profiles |
| **Promo example (Terms reference)** | $4.00/month | Example intro promo when running such programs—not always active |
| **Coach services** | Set by each trainer | Plus **20% Match Fit administrative fee** + card processing at checkout |
| **Admin fee on subscription** | None | 20% fee applies to coach purchases, not monthly subscription |

### Client trials (launch vs standard)

| Segment | Trial | Details |
|---------|-------|---------|
| **Launch cohort clients** (first 50 slots) | **7 days** | Card on file; `LAUNCH_7D` plan |
| **Standard new clients** | **72 hours** | `STANDARD_72H` plan |
| **Pay now** | No trial | Immediate billing option |

Trial reminder emails at 48h and 24h before end (product feature).

### Trainer pricing

| Item | Amount | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Registration fee** | **$100** | Background check payment credited toward this total |
| **Background check** | ~**$49** (configurable via env) | Plus admin + processing fees at checkout |
| **Launch cohort trainers** (first 10 slots) | Background check + fees only | **$100 registration balance waived**; **14 days complimentary Premium Page** |
| **Trainer Premium** | **$20/month** | Featured eligibility, content tools, algorithm boost, unlimited client approaches |
| **Coach service revenue** | Trainer-set | Platform takes 20% admin fee on marketplace purchases |

### Fee transparency (messaging)

- Be explicit: monthly subscription ≠ coach session purchases
- Card processing fees apply to all charges, shown at checkout
- Trainers keep voice, pricing, and delivery model; clients keep agency over who they trust

## 7. Service modalities (what coaches can sell)

Marketing should reflect the breadth without implying Match Fit is a gym or employs coaches.

1. **Mobile & in-person** — trainer meets client at home, office, park, etc. (gym guest passes are client/trainer responsibility)
2. **Virtual sessions** — Zoom, Microsoft Teams, or Google Meet links shared in-app chat
3. **DIY workout programs** — trainer-built plans with SLA around first delivery; ongoing cadence packages
4. **Nutrition plans** — **certified nutritionists only**
5. **Catalog service types** — 1:1 PT, small group (2–8), nutrition coaching, online program design, sport-specific, mobility/recovery, HIIT, yoga/pilates-style

Billing templates include per session, per hour, multi-session bundles, per-person (small group), and cadence packages (weekly/monthly).

## 8. Competitive positioning

### Alternatives clients might consider

- Gym personal training desks
- Instagram/TikTok coach discovery
- Generic directories (Yelp, Google, ClassPass-adjacent)
- Friends/referrals
- Other fitness marketplaces (often static listings)

### Match Fit differentiators to emphasize

1. **Swipe-first intent** — emotional, fast filtering vs static search
2. **Two-sided workflow** — interest queue for trainers, not just lead forms
3. **Integrated economics** — subscription for access + marketplace for services
4. **FitHub** — content layer that builds trust pre-booking
5. **AI-assisted matching** — beyond one-time intake forms
6. **Compliance-forward supply** — background checks and credential verification for trainers
7. **On-platform trust** — chat, payments, session policies, payout protections

### Positioning statement (draft for marketing refinement)

*For people who want a fitness coach that actually fits their life, Match Fit is the matchmaking marketplace that combines swipe-based discovery, transparent pricing, and AI-informed pairing—unlike static directories or social media roulette.*

## 10. Launch & growth mechanics (built into product)

- **50 client slots** — launch cohort: 7-day trial, card on file

### Featured / premium growth loops

- Premium trainers: raffle entries, placement bids, token economy, FitHub promotions

### Retention hooks

- Daily questionnaire (matching + engagement)

- New trainer alerts
- FitHub feed

### Referral / viral (current state)

## 14. Key entities & user journeys (simplified)

### Client journey

1. Land on homepage → understand value → sign up
2. Subscribe ($10/mo) with trial if eligible
3. Complete match preferences + optional daily questionnaire
4. Swipe/browse trainers → save / pass
5. Trainer accepts inquiry or nudges → official chat
6. Purchase coach package (Stripe checkout) → book sessions or DIY engagement
7. Engage FitHub, leave reviews, manage billing

### Trainer journey

1. Sign up → onboarding questionnaire + compliance (TOS, W-9, background check, certs)
2. Publish services → activate dashboard
3. Receive interest / discovery matches → chat (message caps for non-premium on initial outreach)
4. Sell services, deliver sessions, FitHub content
5. Optional Premium ($20/mo) + tokens + featured placements
